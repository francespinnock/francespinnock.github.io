Frances Pinnock (b.1990 Bristol, UK) lives and works in London. In 2024 she recieved the Ingram Prize. Recent solo exhibitions include: _Light Sleeper_, Lightbox Gallery & Museum, Woking, 2025; _Accoutrements & Illuminations_, Sarah Myerscough Gallery, London, 2025. Recent group exhibitions include: _Together_, Sarah Myerscough Gallery, London, 2025; _FOG Design+Art_, San Francisco, with Sarah Myerscough Gallery, London, UK, 2025; _Ingram Prize_, Unit 1 Gallery | Workshop, London, UK, 2024; _This Country_, Crafts Council Gallery, London, UK, 2024; _TEFAF Maastricht_, with Sarah Myerscough Gallery, London, UK, 2024; _Cut and Fold_, Make Hauser & Wirth, Somerset, UK, 2023; _Material Beings_, Cromwell Place, London, UK, 2023; _Hung, Drawn & Quartered_, Standpoint Gallery, London, UK, 2023.  
Her work is held in the permanent collections of the Ingram Collection, UK, and the Museum of Leathercraft, UK.

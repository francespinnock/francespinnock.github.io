(b. 1990,Bristol, UK) lives and works in London. Recent solo exhibitions include: Light Sleeper, Lightbox Gallery & Museum, Woking (2025), Accoutrements & Illuminations, Sarah Myerscough Gallery, London (2025). Recent group exhibitions include: Together, Sarah Myerscough Gallery, London (2025), FOG Design & Art, San Francisco (2025), Ingram Prize, Unit 1 Gallery | Workshop, London (2024), This Country, Crafts Council Gallery, London (2024), TEFAF Maastricht, Maastricht (2023), Cut and Fold, Make Hauser & Wirth, Somerset (2023), Material Beings, Cromwell Place, London (2023), Hung, Drawn & Quartered, Standpoint Gallery, London (2023).
